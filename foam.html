Belegarth is elvish for "Mighty People." Belegarth Medieval Combat Society is is a foam weapon fighting game. You see when you do somthing like this, for safety's sake, you either have to pad the player or you can pad the weapon. As it turns out Padding the weapon is a whole lot cheaper by a couple thousand dollars. Plus our weapons are a less expensive as well since we make them from foam and PVC as opposed to steel.

Dagorhir is Norse for "Battle Lords" Dagorhir Battle Games is much like Belegarth MCS except you need to sign a contract to call yourselves a Dagorhir group and advertise on their Bullitin Board.

Groups from both Belegarth and Dagorhir attend the national events together fight together and generaly get along fairly well.

Saftey: Saftey is very important to us. We in no way wish for anybody playing to get hurt, so yes we do have some basic rules regarding saftey like: No hitting to the head. You shouldn't be able to feel the core of a weapon through the padding. Weapons should be padded sufficently enough so that you can hit someone with full force and not hurt them.
